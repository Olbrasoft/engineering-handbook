## Summary of Phases

1. **Discovery** - What/Why?
2. **Exploration** - Where?
3. **Clarifying Questions** - Resolve unknowns.
4. **Architecture Design** - How? (Compare approaches)
5. **Implementation** - Build & Test.
6. **Quality Review** - Verify.
7. **Summary** - Document.

**Goal:** High quality, consistent architecture, no ambiguity.
